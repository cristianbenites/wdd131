Loopy Cookies Site Plan

Site Name

Loopy Confeitaria

This is the name of a confectionary focused on cookies production. The name is to play on the idea of being a creative and dynamic company.

Site Purpose

Offer an online catalog where customers can view all cookie flavors, with photos and descriptions, having the possibility of selecting the ones they want and forwarding them to WhatsApp, where they can complete the ordering process.

Scenarios

  • What cookie flavors are present in the catalog?
  • Can I see pictures of the cookies?
  • What is the company's contact information?
  • How can I place orders?
